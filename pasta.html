Pasta

Description

This is a world known recipe prepared for its simplicity and taste.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Spaghetti
  • Seasoning
  • Eggs/meat/fish
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Pour an amount of water in a cooking pot and boil
  2. Add some spaghetti, and mix for a few minutes
  3. Transfer the spaghetti into a new pot
  4. Add some fried eggs/ meat/ fish, and seasoning and stir
  5. Pour the mixture in a pan filled with a little amount of oil, and fry for a few minutes
  6. Your pasta should be ready.
