Pancake

Description

This is a world known recipe prepared for its simplicity and taste.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Pour an amount of water in a bowl
  2. Add some flour, and mix
  3. Add some fresh eggs and mix for a fine mixture
  4. Add some sugar and mix well
  5. Pour the mixture in a pan filled with a little amount of oil, and fry.
