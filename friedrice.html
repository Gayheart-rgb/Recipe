Fried rice

Description

This is a world known recipe prepared for its simplicity and taste.

Ingredients

  • cooked rice
  • Salt
  • Seasoning
  • Eggs/meat/fish
  • Oil
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Pour the cooked rice into a cooking pan filled with a little amount of oil
  2. Add some seasoning and vegetables, and mix
  3. Add some fried eggs/meat/fish and stir
  4. Add some salt and mix well
  5. After a few minutes, your delicious fried rice will be ready.
